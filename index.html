Carmesim is a hybrid game designed for curious minds. By combining a physical board experience with an interactive companion app, players explore mechanics that blend traditional tabletop depth with modern technology.

About the Project

Developed with a focus on learning and engagement, Carmesim underwent an extensive testing process. We refined the content and mechanics to ensure a seamless interaction between the board and the screen. Designed for teens and adults, it offers a private, data-free environment for play.

About Us

Carmesim was developed by Ana Beatriz Mota, Camille Luppi, Carolina Momoli, Isabella Brum and Jordana Santos. We are a group of designers and developers dedicated to creating meaningful, accessible experiences that bridge the gap between physical play and digital tools.
