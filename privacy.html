Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Carmesim application, a digital companion for the Carmesim hybrid board game. We respect the privacy of our players and ensure that the digital side of your gaming experience is completely private.

1. Information Collection and Use

The Carmesim app is designed to work offline and locally. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Specifically:

  • No Account Creation: You do not need to create an account or provide an email address to use the app.
  • No Data Collection: We do not collect names, device IDs, or any information about the players.
  • No Analytics or Ads: The app does not contain third-party advertising or analytics tools that track your usage.
  • No Sensor Data Storage: Any camera or sensor interaction required for the hybrid gameplay happens in real-time on your device and is never saved or shared.

2. Third-Party Services

The app operates independently and does not use any third-party services that could identify you. All gameplay logic is processed locally on your device.

3. Younger Players (Children & Teens)

Carmesim is designed for players aged 13 and up. Because the app does not collect any personal information, it provides a safe environment for teenagers and children alike, complying with global privacy standards such as COPPA and GDPR.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if we add new offline features. However, our commitment to not collecting user data will remain the same.
